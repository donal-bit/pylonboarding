Track 02 - Growth

CSAT Champion

Measure quality, set targets, and build feedback loops. By the end of this track you'll have SLA policies, business hours configured, and CSAT surveys catching every customer sentiment signal.

Success: CSAT trending up, SLA compliance above 90%, negative CSAT always gets follow-up
Step 1

Configure SLAs

Clear response time targets improve consistency even without contractual obligations.

  • Urgent: 1hr first response, 4hr resolution
  • High: 4hr first response, 24hr resolution
  • Normal: 8 business hours / 48 business hours
Read the docs →
Step 2

Set Support Hours

SLA timers should pause outside working hours. Define your business hours so the clock only runs when your team is active.

  • Set your timezone and weekly schedule
  • Add holidays and exceptions
  • SLA timers automatically pause outside these hours
Read the docs →

Step 3

Enable CSAT Surveys

Fast doesn't always mean good. CSAT surveys tell you if customers are actually happy with the experience.

  • Enable automatic surveys after issue resolution
  • Send within 1 hour while the experience is fresh
  • Create a "Negative CSAT" View to catch unhappy customers
